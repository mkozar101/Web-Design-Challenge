Visualizing Weather Data

The impact of 'Data' lie in the stories they tell, each with their own characters, narratives and conclusions. Like all great stories in any other medium, data driven ones leverage strong imagery. Imagery, whether physical or mental, has an uncanny ability to draw people in and make a lasting impact. Similar to authors writing a novel, painters drawing a scene, or orators delivering a speech to a crowd, for data scientists, imagery is key to story telling. In the world of data, we often realize this imagery through interactive visualizations, charts and maps.

Weather is driving force of our every day life. It is also well defined by data: what's the temperature? how strong was that hurricane? how many lives were lost to that tornado? Weather affects how much time we spend outside, where we vacation, and in extreme cases, whether our life is in danger. This website is dedicated to telling stories around weather using data from the OpenWeatherAPI. Explore the visualization to gain insight into how geography and weather can play together.
