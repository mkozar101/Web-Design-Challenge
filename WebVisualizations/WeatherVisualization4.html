City Latitude & Wind Speed


Wind is weightless yet it's driven discovery through sailing, energy through wind mills, and inspired countless works of art. But does the experience of wind change depending on your position in the earth? Does latitude drive how the wind blows? It would appear from our data that most cities have wind of less than 20mph on any give day. Interestingly, it appears that the wind speeds increase slightly as latitude increases!

Is there something here? Well with an average wind speed of 50mph across the year, Antarctica, at the very bottom of the world, is one of the windiest places on earth. Is it latitude driving the the story of wind at the pole? Perhaps, but to reach such a conclusion we'll definitely need to do some more data analysis on other factors such as temperature, humidity and cloudiness across that continent!
